Let's Play mini cricket


Rules of the Game

Computer is on the balling side and you are on the batting side.

Computer chooses a random Target runs.

Balls are your chances to smash the score. Each time you get 12 balls.

You have to make selection for number of runs by choosing from 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 runs and 6 runs(thumbs up)

If you reach the target before balls exhaust, you win!

If you reach score but computer matches your selection, hard luck! it's a draw.

If the computer's selection matches your selection, you lose!!
